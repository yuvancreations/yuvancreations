Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our video services

We work with all major video formats including MP4, MOV, AVI, and professional formats like ProRes and DNxHD. For best results, we recommend providing original camera files rather than heavily compressed versions.

Editing time depends on the project complexity. A simple 5-minute edit might take 2-3 days, while a complex 10-minute video with effects could take 1-2 weeks. We'll provide a timeline estimate after reviewing your footage and requirements.

For projects we shoot, we can provide raw footage for an additional fee. For editing-only projects, we typically don't provide project files unless arranged beforehand, as our editing process involves proprietary techniques.

Our standard packages include 2 rounds of revisions. Additional revisions can be purchased if needed. We recommend providing consolidated feedback to make the most of your revision rounds.
