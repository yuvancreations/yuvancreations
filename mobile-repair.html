Frequently Asked Questions

Most common repairs like screen or battery replacements take about 1-2 hours. More complex repairs may take longer, and we'll provide an estimated timeframe after diagnosis.

We use OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) parts whenever possible. For some models, we offer high-quality aftermarket alternatives that meet or exceed original specifications at a lower cost.

We take every precaution to protect your data, but we strongly recommend backing up your device before any repair. Most repairs don't require data access, but unexpected issues can occur.
