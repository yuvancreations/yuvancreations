Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our design services

We provide all standard file formats including JPG, PNG, PDF, and vector files (AI/EPS). For print projects, we deliver CMYK-optimized PDFs with bleed and crop marks. Source files are included in our Standard and Premium packages.

Our Basic package includes 1-2 concepts, Standard includes 3-5 concepts, and Premium includes 5+ concepts. We refine your selected concept through multiple revision rounds until you're completely satisfied.

Yes, all our print designs are delivered as print-ready PDFs with proper bleeds, CMYK color mode, and high resolution. For Premium package clients, we also provide print supervision to ensure colors and quality match your expectations.

Our brand guidelines include logo usage rules, color palette (with CMYK, RGB, HEX, and Pantone values), typography system, spacing rules, and examples of proper and improper usage. Premium packages include more extensive guidelines with application examples.

Absolutely. We can work with your existing logos, colors, and brand elements to create new designs that maintain consistency with your established visual identity. We'll request all relevant brand materials at the start of the project.

Basic projects typically take 1-3 days, Standard projects 3-7 days, and Premium projects 1-2 weeks depending on complexity. Rush services are available for an additional 25-50% fee with turnaround as fast as 24 hours for simple projects.
